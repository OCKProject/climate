Relationship between present-day thermocline depth and the change in IOD properties. ( a) Scatter plot showing the relationship between the present-day mean EEIO thermocline depth and the future-minus-present-day change in IOD amplitude. ( b, c) As in ( a) but for IOD skewness and IO mean gradient, respectively. The diagonal black lines represent the line of best fit and the error bars indicate 1 standard deviation from the multi-member ensemble mean.
