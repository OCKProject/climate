Relationship between thermocline depth and IOD properties. ( a) Present-day (blue circles) and future (red circles) SON relationship between mean eastern equatorial Indian Ocean (EEIO) thermocline depth (ZT) and IOD amplitude. ( b, c) As in ( a) but for IOD skewness and the IO mean gradient, respectively. The diagonal black lines represent the line of best fit and the error bars indicate 1 standard deviation from the multi-member ensemble mean.
