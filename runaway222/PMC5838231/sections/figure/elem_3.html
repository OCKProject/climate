Figure 4

The spatial pattern of SM-P feedback over different soils: ( a) The average correlation with a soil moisture interval of 0.0005 cm 3/cm 3; ( b) Negative feedback in different climate zones. The first letter (A to D) refers to the broad climate type: tropical, arid, temperate or cold. The second letter (f, m, w/W and s/S) represents the subsequent precipitation conditions. The third letter ( a– d, h and k) refers to the temperature classification. The definitions of the climate zones can be found in Peel et al. 46. Each pixel represents an area of 0.25 × 0.25 degrees.
