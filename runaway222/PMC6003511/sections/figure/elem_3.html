Fig. 4.

Model results from the earth system model COSMOS. Results are plotted in all panels as anomalies, calculated as differences resulting from 9 ka minus PI runs. ( A) Annual mean temperature (T) anomaly, shown on a meridional transect along 150° E from the equator to 60° N. The transect crosses directly into the Okhotsk Sea north of 42° N. ( B) Same as in A but for annual mean salinity (S) anomaly. ( C) Sea ice volume anomaly 9 ka minus PI, calculated from sea ice concentration multiplied by sea ice thickness in the North Pacific. ( D) Same as C, but for sea ice thickness.
