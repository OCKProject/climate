Coring and sampling

The present study was conducted on a new borehole Sokli 2010/4 that was cored at a site located between boreholes Sokli A/B-series and 900/901 from the central part of the Sokli basin and 902/905 from the basin margin 21, 22. Additionally, several samples from core 901 were re-analysed for pollen 21. The latter core records the final birch phase that is missing (truncated) at the site of borehole Sokli 2010/4.
