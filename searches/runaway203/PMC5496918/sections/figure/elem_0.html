Figure 1

Increasing seasonality of Arctic sea ice extent. ( a) Annual range of sea ice extent from data and model simulations, ( b) The corresponding seasonality numbers. Monthly NSIDC satellite sea ice index data are shown since 1978 and the Walsh et al. 19 composite of historical data are shown prior to 1978. The PIOMAS estimates are from a data assimilation product 20. The multi-model mean from fifteen CMIP5 coupled climate models are also shown (individual model results appear in Figs  SI4, SI5 and SI6). In ( b) seasonality numbers for NSIDC and HadISST Antarctic sea ice extent are plotted. The thin lines show the seasonality numbers for NSIDC sea ice area. Notice the stretched scale for the period of satellite data.
