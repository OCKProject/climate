Annual Mean Temperature Anomaly (from 1906–2005 mean) in the observed datasets; IITM (black) and CRU (gray), and in the historical (red) and historicalNat (light-blue) simulations with their 5–95% confidence bounds (calculated as in Jones et al. ref. 21) for ( a) All India (ALLIN), and the individual homogeneous zones ( b) West Coast (WCIND), ( c) Interior Peninsula (IPIND), ( d) East Coast (ECIND), ( e) North East (NEIND), ( f) North Central (NCIND), ( g) North West (NWIND), and ( h) Western Himalaya (WHIND). The vertical lines in panels a-h indicate timings of major volcanic eruptions during the 1906–2005 period.The demarcation of homogeneous temperature zones (based on ref. 14) is shown in ( i). The map was generated using UVCDAT 2.12 ( https://uvcdat.llnl.gov/).
