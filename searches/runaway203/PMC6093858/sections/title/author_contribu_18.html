Author contributions

K.M.W.A. conceived of the study and wrote the paper. T.S.v.D. performed AThaw modeling. S.F. created the radiative forcing model; P.A. applied the model and performed statistics. B.J. assisted with contextualizing results related to lake drainage versus expansion. All authors commented on the analysis, interpretation and presentation of the data, and were involved in the writing.
