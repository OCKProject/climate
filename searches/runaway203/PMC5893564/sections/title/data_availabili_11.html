Data availability

Model output from this study is available on Zenodo (zenodo.org) for both GFDL CM2.1 (doi:10.5281/zenodo.1194480) and NCAR CESM (doi:10.5281/zenodo.1194490) data. Annual and monthly climatologies are stored. Monthly data on a fixed-angular calendar is not stored on Zenodo, but is available from the corresponding author upon reasonable request.
