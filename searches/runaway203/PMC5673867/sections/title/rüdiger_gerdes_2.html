Rüdiger Gerdes

is a Senior Scientist in AWI’s Climate Sciences division and Professor of Oceanography at Jacobs University Bremen. He has been principle investigator in several EU projects (VEINS, ASOF-N, CONVECTION, DAMOCLES, Intas NORDIC SEAS, ArcRisk, ACCESS). He is PI in the Arctic Ocean Model Intercomparison Project and a long-time member of the CLIVAR Working Group on Ocean Model Development. His main research interests include atmosphere–sea ice–ocean interaction, Arctic ocean circulation, climate variability, and oceanic teleconnections.
