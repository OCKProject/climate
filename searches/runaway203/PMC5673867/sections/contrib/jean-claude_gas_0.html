Jean-Claude Gascard

is Emeritus Research Director at the Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS) in France. He is a Physical Oceanographer working at the University Pierre & Marie Curie in Paris at the LOCEAN laboratory. He coordinated major EU polar projects such as DAMOCLES during the International Polar Year (IPY) and more recently ACCESS (Arctic Climate Change, Economy and Society). He was past chair of the Arctic Ocean Sciences Board. His main interest is in deep ocean convection, thermohaline circulation, and polar oceans (the Arctic Ocean in particular).
