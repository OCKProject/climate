Availability of data and materials

All national communications reviewed in this study can be accessed from the UNFCCC website (See http://unfccc.int/national_reports/annex_i_natcom/submitted_natcom/items/10138.php). The managed land area data for the United States can be accessed by contacting the US Environmental Protection Agency ( https://www.epa.gov/ghgemissions). For Canada, all cropland (except for ~ 9kha in Yukon), managed grassland and settlements south of 60 N are publicly available ( https://open.canada.ca/en). All other data presented in this report are publicly available based on the references cited. The land database from Brazil can be made available upon request to the General Coordination of Global Climate Change ( http://sirene.mcti.gov.br/).
