Global carbon-climate indicators.

( a) Atmospheric carbon concentration (p.p.m.), shown with CDIAC data 33 and RCP projections 7; ( b) cumulative anthropogenic emissions (2001–2100) [PgC], compared with historical emissions (IPCC 3 and FeliX) and RCP projections. ( c) Total radiative forcing (W m −2) for all greenhouse gases, shown with CDIAC data and RCP projections. CO 2 forcing is modelled endogenously; all other greenhouse gases are exogenously set to RCP 4.5. ( d) Global average surface temperature change relative to preindustrial (Δ T) (°C). Historical time series from HadCRUT4 (ref. 38). Δ T projections associated with each of the RCPs in 2100 are shown at right with 90% confidence intervals 40.
